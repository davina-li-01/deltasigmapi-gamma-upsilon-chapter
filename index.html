General Information About DSP

Delta Sigma Pi is one of the nation’s largest co-ed professional business fraternities. As the Gamma Upsilon Chapter of Delta Sigma Pi, we focus on leadership development, career readiness, and values-based brotherhood at Babson College.

Members gain access to professional workshops, alumni mentors, recruitment preparation, and a network that extends across industries and geographies.
