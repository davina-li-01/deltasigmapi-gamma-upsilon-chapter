Recruitment Process

Our recruitment process is designed to help candidates understand Delta Sigma Pi, meet brothers, and demonstrate values-driven leadership. Each event evaluates professionalism, collaboration, communication, and cultural alignment with the chapter.

Info Session 1Overview of DSP mission, chapter values, semester timeline, and application requirements.
Info Session 2Detailed look at chapter operations, alumni outcomes, and expectations for new members.
Community Service EventParticipate in a hands-on service activity and demonstrate collaborative leadership.
Professional EventAttend a career-focused session featuring networking and business communication exercises.
Social EventEngage with brothers in a casual setting to build connections and learn chapter culture.
Case EventWork in teams to solve a business case and present recommendations to evaluators.
InterviewFinal round focused on motivation, fit, commitment, and growth potential.
